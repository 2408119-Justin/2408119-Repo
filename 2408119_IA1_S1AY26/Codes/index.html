Usain Bolt is my hero because of his unmatched determination, discipline, and drive to be the best. He proved that with hard work, confidence, and persistence, you can rise above challenges and break limits. His success on the track inspires me to push past my own boundaries in life.

Personal Development - Twelve Pillars Reflection

This is a summary of my understanding of the twelve pillars of personal development.

Chapter 4 - Achieve Your Goals

This chapter emphasizes the importance of setting clear, measurable goals. I've learned that breaking down my dream of becoming a software engineer into smaller steps (such as mastering HTML/CSS and building projects) makes success more achievable.

Chapter 5 - The Proper Use of Time

Time is a resource we cannot replace. I sometimes procrastinate, but this chapter challenged me to plan my days better. I now dedicate fixed hours for coursework and self-study.

Chapter 6 - Surround Yourself with the Best People

The people we associate with influence our growth. For me, this means surrounding myself with classmates who are ambitious and engaging with tech communities that challenge me to improve.

Chapter 7 - Be a Lifelong Learner

Learning never ends. This chapter connects to computing perfectly, because technology evolves every day. I've committed to online courses and coding challenges to ensure I keep growing.
